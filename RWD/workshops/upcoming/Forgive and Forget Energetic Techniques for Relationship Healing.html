    Forgive and forget: Relationship healing

    Topic

    Energetic healing techniques

    Lecturer

    Susan Dwayne, Kate Black, Irw and Sarah Wood, Allan and Monika Moon

    Date

    14-15/10/2017

    Place

    Room 2

    Description:

    Learn the Energetic Approach to creating fulfilling relationships. By undertanding how our thoughts and emotions affect our energy interactions, we can easily release anger and cultivate loving heart energies! Practice simple but powerful exercises to release resentment & anger, followed by a powerful meditaiton to awaken the heart connection in all our relationships.

    The first day will be an introduction to the topic. We will both have experts talking about they experience and have an discussion about our own feelings. The day will be ended with a mediation. During the second week we will focus on acting. We will release the resentment and anger, meditate and practice the short exercises which will help you to keep the anger away in the future.
