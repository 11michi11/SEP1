I AM nature within you

Topic Meditations and breathing techniques
Lecturer Sol Gogh, Octavia Friday, Merida and Travis Smith
Date 12-13/08/2017
Place Room 4
Description

You will learn specialized meditations and breathing techniques to precisely tune-in and access the reservoir of blessings of your Higher Soul! The down pouring of Divine Energies from this direct connection will quickly flush out stress, anger, negative emotions and psychic intrusions. Over more during the speeches you will get to know your Higher Soul and learn how to take care of it in your everyday routine.

Master Co will guide you in using your hands to accurately scan and tap in to these Divine Energies for healing, clarity, divine oneness, and more.

Come away filled with inner peace, stillness and divine bliss as Master Stephen Co helps you personally access the tremendous love, light and power of the "I Am" nature (Higher Soul) within you.
