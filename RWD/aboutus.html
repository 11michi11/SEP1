About Us

*THE SOUL USUALLY KNOWS WHAT TO DO TO HEAL ITSELF. THE CHALANGE IS TO SILENCE THE MIND*


Vipassana means to see things as they really are. It's one of India's most ancient techniques of meditation.It was taught in India more than 2500 years ago as a universal cure for illnesses and depressions, i.e., an Art of Living.


COURSES

The technique of Vipassana Meditation is taught at ten-day residential courses where participants learn the basic methods and how to practice them. In the end you will get experience with beneficial results.
