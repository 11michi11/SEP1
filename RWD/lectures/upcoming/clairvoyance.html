Higher Clairvoyance

Topic Higher Clairvoyance
Lecturer Peter Spreech
Date 30/09/2017
Place Room 2
Description

The Higher Clairvoyance is offered to those interested in developing the ability to see beyond the physical material world. Clairvoyance is referred to the ability to see the energy body, the chakras and other subtle vehicles.

The lecturer, Peter Spreech, will provide the principles of developing the “Inner Eyes,” which in esoteric sciences is often called the “Heaven Eyes.” Moreover, Peter will teach you techniques that will enable you to develop your clairvoyance skills with the intention of exposing the way how to see beyond the physical realm and apply it in healing.

"Being a clairvoyant is a gift. It gives you freedom and ability to see what is normally not known to all, the ability to look at objects, happenings and situations from a deeper level, beyond the physical manifestations. It therefore takes you nearer to the truth! This lecture is highly recommended for healers, spiritual practitioners and anyone who seeks the truth."
