    Achieving oneness with the Higher Soul

    Topic

    Achieving oneness with the Higher Soul

    Lecturer

    Kate Smith

    Date

    28/10/2017

    Place

    Room 3

    Description:

    Don't hesitate and feel free to sign up for this lecture if you have ever thought about God, life and existence, definitely questions have strike your mind like who I am, where I have come from, what the purpose of my life is and where I will go after death.

    To answer the above questions, there must be a certain degree of Soul Realization as far as our lecturer, Kate Smith, is concerned. Soul realization is in fact a complex statement since there are different stages of soul realization from intellectual understanding that we are the soul, not the body to actual experience of oneness with God.

    Achieving Oneness with the Higher Soul has been designed for those who are interested in pursuing a spiritual path as it accelerates the evolutionary process of the soul. Its purpose is basically to fasten the union of the Incarnated Soul (often called the personality or lower self) with the Higher Soul (Higher Self). This is also known as soul realization.

    "Although we look different, we have different professions and ways of life, we are all beings of light who knowingly or unknowingly are trying to evolve and to realize our true nature. Achieving Oneness with the Higher Soul makes this possible!"
