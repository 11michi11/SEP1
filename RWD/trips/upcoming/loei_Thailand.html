    Organizer

    Description:

    This compound surrounded by lush tropical forest and rolling green hills in northeastern Thailand is adjacent to Phu Ruea National Park.

    The practice is centered around Dhammakaya meditation which focuses on the center of the body as a gateway to higher consciousness. The property features indoor and outdoor meditation space, waterfall-fed gardens, outdoor dining and a cluster of wooden bungalows, with accommodation separated for men and women.
